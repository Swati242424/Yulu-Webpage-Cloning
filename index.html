How to Yulu



Download YULU app
Download the Yulu app from the Appstore or the Playstore
Locate a Yulu
Use the Yulu app to find the closest vehicle to you or look out for a Yulu Zone around you.
Scan the QR code
To unlock the vehicle, simply scan the QR code located on the panel.
Ride Safely
Enjoy the Yulu ride but remember to abide by all the traffic laws and parking policies.
End your ride
To end your ride, park the vehicle at a Yulu Zone, lock it and click on the End button on your app.

Yulu in News

Yulu launches electric mobility service in Pondicherry, expanding to tenth Indian city

Autocar Pro,August 16,2024


Yulu, an electric vehicle (EV) mobility technology company in India, has launched its service in Pondicherry in partnership with Green Drive Pondicherry, a local venture. The launch event at Pondy Marina was attended by several government officials, including the Chief Minister of Puducherry.


Yulu Bikes aiming to raise over $100 million to fund expansion

Economics Times, August8,2024


Yulu Bikes aims to raise over $100 million in a Series C round to expand its operations and fleet of electric two-wheelers. The company is looking at more than doubling the fleet of electric two-wheelers it deploys for passenger as well as cargo movement to 80,000 by the end of this fiscal year.


Bajaj Auto-powered electric mobility company Yulu eyes global ride

Business Standard,July 29,2024


India’s largest shared electric vehicle (EV) mobility company Yulu is now eyeing foreign shores with its EV two-wheeler ecosystem around 2025-26, powered by a new product from the Bajaj stable, besides expanding into more markets and product categories in India.
